ABOUT

Ayurveda is an ancient medical science which was developed in India thousands of years ago. Ayurvedic treatements are based on three Doshas - Vata Dosha, Pitta Dosha, Kapha Dosha. At Maulee Ayurveda, the goal of the treatements provided, is to balance all these three doshas by the process of "panchakarma".

Dr. Urmila Patil [B.A.M.S.] has been consulting since 16 years in Mumbai. She has great knowledge in field of infertility and has consulted 40 to 50 infertility cases that have been successful. With the help of Nadi Parikshan, she can cure many diseases.

At Maulee Ayurveda, Dr. Urmila Patil is actively present in making all the medicines by herself for her patients. Her self made medicines gives her power to treat patients confidently. She is also fully devoted and committed to her patients.

During the establishment of Maulee Ayurveda, founder Rajesh Sankhe had a vision, that our ancient medical science; Ayurveda, should be used in our daily lives and know it's importance.

In modern times, we have accepted and have become dependent on different "pathies" and hence have forgotten our ancient ayurvedic medicine. With the help of Maulee Ayurveda, Rajesh Sankhe wants us to accept ayurvedic science in our daily life and to live a happy and healthy life.
